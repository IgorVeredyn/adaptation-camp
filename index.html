Adaptation Camp

📅 Date: March 7-9, 2024

Adaptation Camp is a 3-day integration event for 100-200 Erasmus students from Gdańsk, Bydgoszcz, Olsztyn & Szczecin. You will be divided into 6 teams and compete like in Hogwarts! 🫐🍒🍇🥒🍌🥕

The best recommendation of Adaptation Camp is that for 5 years, this trip has won the award for the best Erasmus event in Poland... every year!

The price (~€99 first minute) includes:

  • Accommodation 🏠
  • Meals 🥞🍲🍝
  • Team T-shirt 👕
  • 2 parties 💃🕺
  • Happy hour with open bar 🍸🥂
  • Sauna 👳‍♀
  • Bowling & billiards 🎳🎱
  • Gadgets & lots of integration games 🥳
  • Special game where you can win a free trip 🏆
